# Deleting your Sonos Dialoger Account

To request the deletion of your Sonos Dialoger Account, please send a email to
flavianzullig@gmail.com.

Regularly, your account and all of the information associated with it are deleted within 24 hours of
your inquiry.
If that is not the case, please feel free recontact me.

Data that will be deleted:

- Your email
- Your password
- Your role in the app
- Your account

We do not store any information about your account other than those.
